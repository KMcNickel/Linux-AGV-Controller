8BitDo Pro 2

Axes:
0 - Left LR (R Pos)
1 - Left UD (D Pos)
2 - L Trig (Pressed Pos)
3 - Right LR (R Pos)
4 - Right UD (D Pos)
5 - R Trig (Pressed Pos)
6 - D Pad LR (R Pos)
7 - D Pad UD (D Pos)

Buttons:
0 - B
1 - A
2 - Y
3 - X
4 - L
5 - R
6 - Select
7 - Start
8 - P2 (Assigned to LS)
9 - P1 (Assigned to RS)

Note: P2 is on the left
